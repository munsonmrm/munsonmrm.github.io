OUR STORY

Blank Bakery was created by someone who needed to make a website that coincidentally has always wanted to own a bakery. She honestly doesn't really know what she wants to do with her life in general, but here she can play around and see what her dream establishment might look like with some of her favorite recipes (even if only in code).
