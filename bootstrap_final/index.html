Hello!

I’m Madison Munson and I am a videographer currently based in Harrisonburg, VA. I love to create and experiment with all types of digital media, but I specialize in video editing & cinematography.

From an early age my Nana exposed me to the world of art by setting up a craft table any time I’d visit and taking me to local art exhibitions and performances. My love to create was born then, and I’ve now honed my artistic passion into digital media. When I’m not editing or designing, you can find me baking pastries, playing video games, or taking a nice long walk out in nature.
