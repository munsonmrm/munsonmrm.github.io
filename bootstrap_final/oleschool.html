OLE SCHOOL PROMO

Project Type

Promotional Video

Tools Used

Canon C70, IKAN Lighting kit, Boom Mic, Final Cut Pro

Role

Director, Co-Cinematographer, Editor

Year

2022

This video was made to promote James Madison University's Ole School Alumni Group.

The process of making this video involved interviewing our subject, piecing together a profile of their life that emphasized Ole School's impact, and shooting footage to help flesh out the story. While on the shorter side, this video is one I am most proud of.
